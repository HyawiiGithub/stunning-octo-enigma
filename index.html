The Zone

Something went
wrong with the signal.

Signal Breakers is a co-op extraction horror game on Roblox. You and your team enter procedurally generated "signal zones" — corrupted environments where physics breaks, reality loops, and things that shouldn't exist have started paying attention.

Collect Signal Fragments. Carry limited gear. Make hard calls. Get out before the zone collapses.

Every run is different. Every anomaly is unpredictable. Every entity learns.

Entity Database

You're not
alone.

Signal zones generate hostile entities. Each behaves differently. None of them are friendly.

E-01
The Watcher
Stands motionless. Kills players who look directly at it. Moves only when unobserved. You never see it move.
Threat
E-02
The Hollow
Fast. Low to the ground. Activates in darkness and deactivates in light. Carries no sound.
